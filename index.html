Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Oya Portraits is a free, non-commercial educational resource created by Association Oya: Conservation for the Next Generation, Geneva, Switzerland.

What data we collect

Oya Portraits does not collect, store, or share any personal data about its visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools.

Third-party services

The app makes read-only requests to the following services to display species information and photographs:

  • IUCN Red List (iucnredlist.org) — conservation status and species data, used under the IUCN Terms of Use for non-commercial educational purposes.
  • iNaturalist (inaturalist.org) — Creative Commons-licensed photographs of species.
  • GBIF (gbif.org) — georeferenced species occurrence data for maps.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles via Leaflet.js.

These services have their own privacy policies. Oya Portraits does not transmit any user data to these services.
